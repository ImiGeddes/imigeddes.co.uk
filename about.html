A BIT ABOUT ME...

Design to me is connecting human behaviour with creative solutions.

The combination of phsychology, research and creativity is what drew me to UX design.

Three things I love are nature, travelling and learning new skills.

A VERY BRIEF HISTORY...
 
CHILDHOOD

I grew up in Surrey (although I like to say “basically London”). At school I developed a fascination for two things; the environment and design.

UNIVERSITY

I went to The University of Leeds and studied Sustainabiliy and Environmental Management, which I loved! I concentrated on behaviour change, which is where I developed my interest in creating solutions to help people and the environment.

CONSULTANCY

I became a Sustainability Consultant soon after I graduated. During my 2 years here I worked with many different people to understand their sustainability challenges and create solutions to solve them. Ultimately I decided that what I really wanted to do was design and research people’s behaviour.

LIVING ABROAD

I decided before I left my consultancy job that I wanted to be a Product Designer, however I took the opportunity inbetween my career change to do something I always wanted to do: work abroad. So I booked a one way ticket to Buenos Aires and worked there as an English Teacher.

UX & UI DESIGN DIPLOMA

Having done a tonne of research, reading and attending events I took the plunge and booked onto a UX & UI Design bootcamp at RED Academy. This was an incredible 6 months of intense work and learning, in both London and Vancouver.

CURRENTLY

I'm currently designing digital products that makes allows everyone to access their own source of clean energy at Ripple Energy. I also do some occasional freelance projects.
